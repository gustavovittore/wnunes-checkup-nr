## Style Direction

- Corporate
- Trustworthy
- Professional
- Safety-focused

## Visual Notes

- Avoid playful or childish design
- Avoid neon colors
- Keep clean and modern layout
- Use green as primary highlight color
- Use light green for subtle backgrounds or states
- Maintain strong contrast for readability
